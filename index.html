Web Hosting

Web hosting is a service that allows individuals or organizations to make their website accessible on the internet. When you build a website, the files that make up your website (like HTML, CSS, JavaScript files, images, etc.) need to be stored on a server that is connected to the internet. Web hosting providers offer these servers along with the necessary technologies and services required to make your website accessible online.

Responsive Designs

A responsive website is one that automatically adjusts its layout and design to provide an optimal viewing experience across a variety of devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. The goal of responsive design is to ensure usability and readability on different screen sizes without the need for horizontal scrolling or zooming.
